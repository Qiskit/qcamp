What is included in the complimentary transportation and accommodation?

Transportation will be provided to the Mountain Top Inn & Resort in Killington, VT on February 26. Return transportation will be provided back to NYC AND BOSTON on March 1.

What will the accommodations look like?

You'll be hackin' and relaxin' at the Mountain Top Inn & Resort in Killington, Vermont. We'll be providing a few different options for lodging which you can check out here:

  • Cabins
  • Rooms

Is there a dress code for this event?

There is no formal dress code for this event. We do recommend bundling up as wintertime in Vermont isn't necessarily known for it's beach weather.

Are you interested in winter activities?

Killington, VT is known for world class skiing conditions. While we will not be providing ski passes there will be complimentary shuttles to and from the resort for you to indulge in some winter sports action!

Is this event open for plus one guests?

Plus ones and families are welcome. If you are attending this event with guests please let us know in order to arrange appropriate accommodation*.

* Please note familiar accommodations are limited, and will be assigned on a first come, first served basis.
