What is included in the complimentary transportation and accommodation?

Transportation from the IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Center to the Mountain Top Inn & Resort in Chittenden, VT will be provided by IBM. Our motorcoaches will leave the IBM building in Yorktown immediately after the event on February 26th, and will stop in Troy, NY for dinner before dropping us off at the Mountaintop Inn.

At the conclusion of the Vermont portion of the event, transportation from the Mountain Top Inn & Resort will be provided to the following drop off locations:

  • Grand Central Station in NYC
  • IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Facility in Yorktown Heights, NY
  • South Station in Boston

Once you are registered for the event, we will send you a follow-up email with more details on lodging and transportation to ensure that your needs are covered.

Questions? Email hello@qiskit.camp

Lodging and accommodation

The Qiskit team will make reservations at the Mountain Top Inn & Resort for all participants registered for the 2019 Qiskit Camp.

  • IBM will cover up to 3 nights of lodging (Room & Tax only) for all pre-approved students: Tuesday February 26th, Wednesday February 27th, and Thursday February 28th)
  • Incidental charges (bar, room service, phone calls, gift shop, etc.) will need to be covered by each attendee at checkout
  • Accommodations outside of the dates listed above are to be arranged directly by the participant.

What will the accommodations look like?

Is there a dress code for this event?

There is no formal dress code for this event. We do recommend bundling up as wintertime in Vermont isn't necessarily known for it's beach weather.

Are you interested in winter activities?

Killington, VT is known for world class skiing conditions. While we will not be providing ski passes there will be complimentary shuttles to and from the resort for you to indulge in some winter sports action!

Is this event open for plus one guests?

Plus ones and families are welcome. If you are attending this event with guests please let us know in order to arrange appropriate accommodation*.

* Please note familiar accommodations are limited, and will be assigned on a first come, first served basis.
