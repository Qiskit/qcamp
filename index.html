What is Q Camp?

Apply to join us for the first Qiskit community experience this February. The event includes a 1 day invitation to IBM Q Research headquarters before embarking to a mountain resort where we will host a 24-hour hackathon competition in a barn, enjoy winter campfires, and many other surprises.

When & Where?

Feb 26
IBM Research Headquarters
Yorktown Heights, NY

One day conference with talks and training sessions with the Qiskit core team, including a visit to the lab to see real quantum computers.

Feb 27-Mar 1
Mountain Top Resort
Killington, VT

24-hour hackathon, awards, after-party and winter snow activities. Transportation, accommodation*, entertainment and food is included.

* The Mountain Top Resort consists of cabins where hackathon team members can opt in to be co-located in shared cabin space with priority given to students or others receiving travel assistance. A limited number of individual accommodations will be available or we ask that you make your own bookings.

How to apply?

This is an invitation-only event. Students, researchers, educators, and developers are all encouraged to apply.

Applications will be reviewed and judged based on contributions to the Qiskit and broader quantum community.
